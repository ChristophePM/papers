MJEPA: A Simple and Scalable Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture for Audio-Visual Learning
Self-Supervised Learning Audio-Visual Teotia et al. · FAIR at Meta & NYU · 2025
A single shared ViT encoder for audio and video using cross-modal JEPA prediction. Frozen ViT-g beats prior baselines by +6.8 mAP on AudioSet-20K — trained from scratch, no ImageNet pretraining.
